The Best Movies According to Aditya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Avengers (2012)

Earth's greatest heroes unite to save the world from an otherworldly threat in this action-packed ensemble spectacle.

Avatar (2009)

On the lush moon of Pandora, a paraplegic Marine's journey into an avatar becomes a battle between human greed and the planet's spiritual essence.

Men in Black (1997)

Agents J and K, part of a secret agency policing extraterrestrial activity, keep Earth safe from aliens in this stylish and humorous sci-fi adventure.
